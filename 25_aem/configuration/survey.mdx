This document describes the format and content for configuring your survey data.

<Info>
When defining your survey, you can choose to specify either the individual survey lines you want to run or the area you want to invert. However, you can only provide one of these options, not both.
</Info>

Choosing Between Individual Lines or Survey Area

Here's a breakdown of the two approaches:

Individual Lines: Define each survey line using a unique identifier and its starting and ending coordinates.
Survey Area: Specify the southwest and northeast corner coordinates to define the rectangular area for your survey.
